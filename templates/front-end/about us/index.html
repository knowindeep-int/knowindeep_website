WHO ARE WE?

We are a group of young and enthusiastic undergrads trying to solve a problem every programmer or anyone who has tried his hands with languages or technical buildup ever faces. We have tried to explain things in their most raw form but making practical applications easier.

WHAT DO WE DO??

We believe that the best way to learn anything is through application and we aim to provide that through hands-on projects and their logic and coding tutorials in various technical fields such as web development, app development, image processing, cybersecurity, GitHub, and competitive programming and many more to come, thus, enabling our users to create an everlasting skill to provide them with a working skill and not just some theoretical knowledge. You apply, you learn. Apart from this, we also teach the basics of programming under various modules and enable our users to understand each and every piece of stuff they see on KnowInDeep.
