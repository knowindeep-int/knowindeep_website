Content Creation and Submission Guidelines

In case the author seeks to submit a product of his/her own choice, the author must first have confirmation from KnowInDeep whether he/she can work on that project. The confirmation will be provided after it is verified that the project is of suitable difficulty level, appropriate field, and the project is not already made by or being created by any other author for KnowInDeep.

In case the author does not have any project to work on, he/she can ask for the project to work on from the suitable people associated with KnowInDeep having clearance for assigning projects to anyone.

Any project created by the author must follow the Project Guidelines that will be shared with the author after the author gets confirmation for the project. In case the Project Guidelines are not shared with the author, the author must ask for them before proceeding to avoid any discrepancies.

Payment

KnowInDeep reserves complete right to decide whether a product(project) will be paid or unpaid on any of its platform(s) and if paid, the price at which the project will be sold. Payments to the author will be made on the sale of any of his paid products available on the website only. The payment will be based on the number of products sold made by the author as per the following set of rules:

The following set of rules applies to the sale of a project:

  • For each sale of the author’s paid project, they will initially be paid 40% of the project’s price valid for the user after discounts and before applicable taxes up to the first 30 sales.
  • For the next 30 sales (31st to 60th sale), the author will be paid 30% of the price of the project valid for the user after discounts and before applicable taxes.
  • For any further sale (61st onwards), the author will be paid 20% of the price of the project valid for the user after discounts and before applicable taxes.
  • The payment process for a project is applicable up to 18 months of that product(up to 18 months after the product is live on any of our platform(s)). After 18 months, KnowInDeep will have complete authority and copyright of your product and any other payments for that product will not be made independent of the sale of the product in the initial 18 months.

The following set of rules applies to the sale of a project series:

  • For each sale of the author’s paid project series, they will be paid 40% of the price of the project valid for the user after discounts and before applicable taxes.
  • The payment process for a project series is applicable up to 4 years of that product(up to 4 years after the product is live on any of our platform(s)). After 4 years, KnowInDeep will have complete authority and copyright of your product and any other payments for that product will not be made independent of the sale of the product in the first 4 years.
